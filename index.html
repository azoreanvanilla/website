Vanilla & Orchid Plantation — São Miguel, Azores

Experimentally cultivating vanilla orchids in greenhouse conditions on São Miguel island. Competing with the renowned pineapple plantations of the Azores, we offer a unique plantation experience focused on vanilla and orchids.

Project

This project explores whether vanilla and orchid (orchidaceae) can thrive in greenhouse conditions on São Miguel Island. We combine small-scale R&D, sustainable off-grid methods, and agrotourism to build a unique product and visitor experience. While the Azores are famous for their pineapple plantations, our focus is on vanilla orchid plantations—bringing a new specialty to the region and offering an alternative plantation experience.

Azores Plantations: Pineapple & Vanilla Orchids

The Azores are world‑known for pineapple plantations—an iconic local experience. Baunilha dos Açores adds a complementary option: a vanilla orchid plantation focused on greenhouse cultivation, research, and education.

  • Tradition & Heritage: Pineapple plantations are a historic Azorean success story.
  • New Specialty: Vanilla is an orchid, and greenhouse control makes it possible to cultivate it reliably on São Miguel.
  • Visitor Experience: Both are great plantation visits—ours highlights pollination biology, climate control, and open data.
