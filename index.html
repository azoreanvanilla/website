Azores Plantations: Pineapple & Vanilla Orchids

The Azores are world‑known for pineapple plantations—an iconic local experience. Baunilha dos Açores adds a complementary option: a vanilla orchid plantation focused on greenhouse cultivation, research, and education.

  • Tradition & Heritage: Pineapple plantations are a historic Azorean success story.
  • New Specialty: Vanilla is an orchid, and greenhouse control makes it possible to cultivate it reliably on São Miguel.
  • Visitor Experience: Both are great plantation visits—ours highlights pollination biology, climate control, and open data.
